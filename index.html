💿 DiskDigger-Desktop - Recover Your Deleted Files Quickly

Download DiskDigger for Windows

💡 Overview

DiskDigger-Desktop helps you find and restore lost files from your computer. If you accidentally delete a photo, document, or video, this tool scans your storage drives to find traces of your data. It works on Windows 10 and Windows 11. The software ignores the file system and looks at the raw sectors of your disk to locate hidden information.

⚙️ System Requirements

Your computer must meet these basic needs to use this software:

  • Operating System: Windows 10 or Windows 11 (64-bit).
  • Storage: At least 50MB of free space on your hard drive.
  • Memory: Minimum of 2GB of RAM.
  • Permissions: You need administrator access to allow the program to scan your hard drive.

🚀 How to Download and Install

Follow these steps to get the software on your machine:

  1. Visit the official download page to see the latest version.
  2. Click the link for the Windows executable file (ending in .exe) to start your download.
  3. Save the file to your desktop or your Downloads folder.
  4. Locate the file and double-click it to run the installer.
  5. Follow the prompts on your screen. Click "Next" to move through the setup process.
  6. Once the progress bar completes, you will see a shortcut icon on your desktop.

🔍 Scanning for Files

When you open the application, you see a list of your connected drives. Choose the drive where you lost your data. If you lost files from an external USB stick or an SD card, connect that device first. Once you select the drive, choose the type of scan you want. A quick scan finds recent files fast. A deep scan looks through the entire drive to find older or damaged files. Click the Start button after you make your selection.

💾 Saving Recovered Data

The program displays a list of files as it finds them. You can click on a file to see a preview. To restore a file, select it and click the Recover button. The software asks you to choose a folder on your computer to save the recovered file. Do not save recovered files to the same drive where you lost them. This protects your original data from being overwritten by the operating system.

🛠️ Troubleshooting Tips

If you encounter issues, check these items:

  • The scan shows no files: Try running the deep scan mode. This takes longer but finds more items.
  • Access denied errors: Right-click the application icon and choose "Run as administrator."
  • Files appear corrupted: This often happens if the original file location was rewritten by new data. Stop using the drive immediately to prevent further damage.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Does this work on memory cards? Yes, the tool supports SD cards, USB drives, and internal hard disks.

Is the process safe? Yes, the program does not change or delete existing files on your drive. It strictly reads the data to perform the recovery.

How long does a scan take? A scan depends on the size of your drive. Large drives may take several hours to scan fully.
